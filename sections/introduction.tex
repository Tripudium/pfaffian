\subsection{Notation an conventions} We denote by $\N$ the set of natural numbers including $0$, and by $\N_{>0}$ the set of positive natural numbers. For $n\in \N$, we use the notation $[n] := \{k\in \N_{>0}\colon k\leq n\}$. For $d,n\in \N$, we denote by $\R[X_1, \ldots, X_n]_{(\le d)}$ the vector subspace of the polynomial ring $\R[X_1,\dots,X_n]$ containing polynomials of degree at most $d$.
